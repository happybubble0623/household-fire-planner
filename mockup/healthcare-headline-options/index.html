Healthcare result headline — three ways to show it

The current headline is an undiscounted lifetime sum (~$710k today’s dollars, ~$1.5M nominal for a couple) — true, but it reads as scarily high and isn’t comparable to numbers people see elsewhere. Below are three clearer headlines plus the today’s-vs-future toggle treatment. Example figures shown for a couple.
