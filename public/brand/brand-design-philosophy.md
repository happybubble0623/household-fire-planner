# Household FIRE Planner — Logo Design Philosophy

## Movement: Quiet Ascent

The mark is built on a single idea — a household rising, calmly and deliberately, toward independence. It speaks through geometry, not decoration: a rooftop that is also a path upward. The form is reduced to its essence so it reads instantly at favicon size and holds its dignity at full scale. Nothing is ornamental; every line earns its place.

## Visual language

- **Form and space.** A soft-cornered green tile holds a white roofline. The roof doubles as motion — an upward chevron, a rising peak, a step toward something better. Generous interior margins let the mark breathe and keep it legible at 16px.
- **Color.** A single brand green (`#15803d`) carries trust, growth, and money's natural association with stability. White provides clarity and contrast. An optional warm spark (`#f5b301`) is the only place heat enters — a restrained nod to "FIRE" that never tips into cliché.
- **Rhythm and scale.** The mark is square and balanced; the wordmark sits beside it with calm spacing. "FIRE" is rendered in the brand green so the eye lands on the one word that defines the audience, without shouting.
- **Restraint.** No gradients, no shadows, no flourish. The craftsmanship is in the proportions — the angle of the roof, the weight of the stroke, the alignment of the wordmark baseline — refined until it feels inevitable.

## Concepts

- **A — Home + rising assets.** A rooftop over three ascending bars. The most literal: a household whose portfolio is growing. Best when clarity of purpose matters most.
- **B — Roof as an upward path.** A single bold chevron resting on a foundation line. The most minimal and scalable; reads as both a home and a step up. Recommended default.
- **C — Roof with a FIRE spark.** Concept B plus a small warm spark above the peak — a subtle, tasteful reference to FIRE for those who know the term.

## Usage

- Primary color: `#15803d`. Knockout: white on green. On dark surfaces, use the white mark on the green tile (unchanged) or an all-white version.
- Minimum size: 16px (favicon). Keep clear space of at least half the tile height around the mark.
- The wordmark sets "Household FIRE Planner" in a clean grotesque (Arial/Helvetica to match the app), with "FIRE" in brand green.
- Tagline (optional, never inside the mark): "Plan your path to financial independence and early retirement — across all your household accounts."
